How to read this table

Rental Income: Expected rental income after vacancy adjustments

Mortgage Expense: Annual mortgage payments (principal + interest)

Other Costs: All ongoing expenses (property management, maintenance, taxes, CapEx)

Net Cash Flow: Annual profit or loss from rental operations

House Value: Estimated property value with appreciation

House Equity: Property value minus remaining mortgage

Wealth (Rent Out): Total wealth if renting until this year then selling

Wealth (Sell Now): Potential wealth if selling now and investing proceeds

Difference in Wealth: How much better (or worse) renting out performs

Note: In the final year (10), selling costs are applied to the "Rent Out" scenario, assuming you would sell the property at that point.
